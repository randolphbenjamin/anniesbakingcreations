Questions?

We have answers

Do you do custom orders?
Absolutely — custom orders are one of the most beloved parts of what Annie does. Weddings, birthdays, holidays, baby showers, and more. Use the order form or reach out via the Contact section to get started.
What are your hours?
Hours are seasonal and may vary week to week. Generally we're open roughly 7am – 6pm during the season. Follow us on Facebook or Instagram for the most up-to-date hours and availability.
Do you deliver?
We are currently pickup only, located in Bassett, WI. See the Contact section below for location details.
